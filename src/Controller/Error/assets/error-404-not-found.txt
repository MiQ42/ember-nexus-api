 Error 404 - Not Found

 The requested element was not found.

 This can happen due to multiple reasons:

 - The requested route does not exist.
 - The requested element did never exist.
 - There is a typo within the element's UUID.
 - You do not have access to the element.
 - You do not have the correct type of access to the element.

 Link to the documentation: https://ember-nexus.github.io/api

 Link to http.dev's explanation of the 404 error code: https://http.dev/404
